About BARISAL

Barisal, known as the "Venice of the East," is a beautiful city in southern Bangladesh, surrounded by rivers and lush greenery. It is famous for its scenic waterways, rich cultural heritage, and vibrant floating markets. The city has a deep historical significance, with landmarks like Durga Sagar and the 19th-century Oxford Mission Church. Barisal is also known for its delicious seafood and unique local delicacies like panta bhat and hilsa fish. With its tranquil environment and warm hospitality, Barisal offers a charming blend of tradition and natural beauty..
